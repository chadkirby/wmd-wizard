## Description

Our Filling & Capping Line (12-30 Nozzles) is a fully automated line with capping & filling capabilities. This automated line is manufactured on customer request. Specs will be based on your production & output requirements.

Our Filling & Capping Line (12-30 Nozzles) is an extremely flexible machine. It can process several formats of containers and caps, allowing for quick changeover.

- Compact Stainless Steel Machine Frame. Adjustable height with lexan safety protections
