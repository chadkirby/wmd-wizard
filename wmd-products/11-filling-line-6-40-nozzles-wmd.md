## Description

Our Volumetric Filling Line is a fully automated filler that can be configured with either Electromagnetic Flow Meters or Pistons. Often used for cosmetics, pharmaceutical, food, chemical, and home care industries. Product viscosity can range from water to thick sauces, creams, lotions, liquids with solids, and more.

This Volumetric Filling Line can be outfitted with 6 to 12 filling nozzles in a linear format, or 8 to 40 nozzles in a rotary format. Filling accuracy: +/- 0.02%.
